Who we are?

At Creaspace, we offer a vibrant Minecraft experience where creativity and community come together. Our server is designed for players who love to build, explore, and engage in a dynamic creative world. Whether you're a seasoned architect or a new explorer, Creaspace provides a supportive and exciting environment to make your mark. Key Features: Custom Gameplay: Enjoy a creative experience with a focus on creativity and collaboration. Flying is disabled to encourage innovative transportation systems and community-driven projects. Active Community: Join a friendly and engaged player base. Participate in server-wide events, contribute to city developments, and interact with other members. Robust Plugins: Utilize powerful plugins like WorldEdit for large-scale building and ImageMaps to display custom images in-game. Organized Structure: Our server features clearly defined roles, organized channels, and detailed information to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience. Why Choose Us? Creaspace is more than just a Minecraft server; it's a community where players support each other, collaborate on ambitious projects, and create lasting memories. With dedicated staff and a focus on player experience, we strive to provide a top-notch environment for all.

Server FAQs.

Welcome to the Creaspace FAQ! Here you’ll find answers to common questions about our Minecraft server. If you have any other queries, feel free to reach out to our support team.

**What version of Minecraft is the server running?**
Our server is currently running on Minecraft version 1.20.6. Ensure you are using this version to connect and enjoy all the features we offer!
**How do I join the server?**
To join Creaspace, you need to apply to be added to our whitelist.
**What are the server rules?**
Our server rules include no griefing, respecting other players’ builds, and following community guidelines. Please review the full rules in the #rules-and-agreements channel on our Discord server for more details.
**How can I get roles on the server?**
Roles are assigned based on your activity, contributions, and engagement in the community. Refer to the #role-explanations channel on our Discord for details on how to earn and request different roles.
